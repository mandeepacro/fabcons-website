Accounts Receivable Outsourcing Services

A 15 year old accounting outsourcing firm providing skilled and experienced accounts receivable specialists at economical rates. Our teams of accounts receivable specialists and accountants have extensive customer billing experience across multiple types of businesses namely retail, real estate, IT, consulting, construction and more. Our accounts receivable personnel’s are well versed with complicated customer billing/invoicing processes such as progressive billing, complex timesheet based billing, commission billing, rent runs (real estate companies) among others. They have high expertise in QuickBooks, Xero, NetSute, Yardi (Genesis and Voyager), AppFolio, ERP and Oracle packages. We have worked with 300+ businesses across US, Canada, UK, Middle East, and Singapore among others.

Accounts Payable Services

1

2 way/3 way matching of purchase orders with invoices and goods receipts/packing slips

2

Allocation of invoices against right job, property or department (for construction and real estate clients)

3

Processing of utility bills in the accounting software

4

Ageing of payables and tracking of payables

5

Weekly Check-run Processing (Only in Sr. Accounts Payable Specialist Role)

6

Travel and Entertainment (T&E) expense reimbursement processing

7

Tracking vendors and sub-contractor records for ensuring compliance to workers insurance and other statutory requirements

8

Issue of 1099s to vendors (only in Sr. Accounts Payable Specialist Role)
